MathJax Test

For inline formulas, enclose the formula in $...$. For displayed formulas, use $$...$$. These render differently. For example, type $\sum_{i=0}^n i^2 = \frac{(n^2+n)(2n+1)}{6}$ to show \(\sum_{i=0}^n i^2 = \frac{(n^2+n)(2n+1)}{6}\) (which is inline mode) or type

$$
\sum_{i=0}^n i^2 = \frac{(n^2+n)(2n+1)}{6}
$$

to show

$$ \sum_{i=0}^n i^2 = \frac{(n^2+n)(2n+1)}{6} $$
